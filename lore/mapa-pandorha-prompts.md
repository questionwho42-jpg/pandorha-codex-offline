# Mapa de Pandorha: Prompts Técnicos em Inglês (Labels em Português)

Este documento contém os prompts otimizados em inglês para melhor performance no Nano Banana, com instruções explícitas para que os textos internos do mapa apareçam em **Português**.

## 🗺️ Guia de Disposição Geográfica

1.  **Floresta dos Ecos:** Extremo Oeste.
2.  **Morden:** Centro-Oeste (Leste da floresta).
3.  **Terras Geladas:** Norte de Morden.
4.  **Draskar:** Sul de Morden.
5.  **Gorbax:** Cordilheira Central (Espinha dorsal de Draskar até as Terras Geladas).
6.  **Dungard:** Leste de Gorbax.
7.  **Cinar:** Sudeste de Dungard.
8.  **Almar:** Extremo Leste (Cobrindo a maior área).
9.  **Rio do Céu:** No centro exato de Almar, cruzando de norte a sul (verticalmente).

---

## 🎨 Versão 1: Clássico/Pergaminho (Estilo A)

> **Prompt:**
> "Epic fantasy map of the world of Pandorha on aged parchment. A massive central mountain range named 'Gorbax' divides the map vertically from North to South. To the FAR WEST, a dense green 'Floresta dos Ecos'. To the EAST of the forest, the city of 'Morden' under a violet dome. To the NORTH of Morden are the 'Terras Geladas' and to the SOUTH the desert of 'Draskar'. The 'Gorbax' mountains start at Draskar and end at the frozen north. To the EAST of the mountains is the high peak of 'Dungard', and to its SOUTHEAST the forest of 'Cinar' with seven crystal towers. The far EAST is occupied by the vast kingdom of 'Almar'. A floating 'Rio do Céu' flows vertically through the exact center of Almar, crossing it from North to South, without touching any borders of other kingdoms. **IMPORTANT: All map labels and titles must be in Portuguese language.** Hand-drawn ink style, sepia tones, intricate details."

## 🛰️ Versão 2: Satélite/Realista (Estilo B)

> **Prompt:**
> "High-resolution satellite view of the continent of Pandorha. A colossal central volcanic mountain range called 'Gorbax' splits the continent from South to North. In the FAR WEST, a dark green expanse of mutant jungle ('Floresta dos Ecos') followed by 'Morden' city bastion with a violet energy shield. NORTH of Morden: white ice sheets ('Terras Geladas'). SOUTH of Morden: red desert sands ('Draskar'). The 'Gorbax' range connects both. To the EAST of Gorbax, the towering 'Dungard' peak and 'Cinar' forest to its southeast. The vast eastern territory is the kingdom of 'Almar'. A floating liquid ribbon ('Rio do Céu') crosses the horizontal center of Almar vertically from North to South, entirely contained within Almar's territory. **IMPORTANT: All text labels on the map must be in Portuguese.** 8k, cinematic lighting, realistic terrain textures."

## ✨ Versão 3: Místico/Mágico (Estilo D)

> **Prompt:**
> "Ethereal mystical map of Pandorha showing magical ley lines. A glowing central spine of fire and stone ('Gorbax') acts as the world's axis. WEST: green chaotic aura of 'Floresta dos Ecos' and the technomagic glow of 'Morden'. NORTH of Morden: cosmic blue light of 'Terras Geladas'. SOUTH of Morden: golden light of 'Draskar'. EAST of the mountains: the star-lit peak of 'Dungard' and the silver constellation of 'Cinar'. The immense vital energy of 'Almar' covers the eastern horizon. The 'Rio do Céu' glows as a vertical line of water crossing the exact center of Almar from North to South, separate from all other borders. **IMPORTANT: Map region names and titles must be written in Portuguese.** Digital art style, vibrant colors, arcane runes, dreamlike illustration."

## 🛠️ Notas de Uso

1.  **Linguagem:** O prompt está em inglês para que o motor da IA entenda melhor as formas e biomas, mas a instrução **"All map labels must be in Portuguese"** garante que os nomes apareçam corretamente.
2.  **O Eixo:** Lembre-se que Gorbax é a "coluna" que divide o mapa entre o complexo Morden/Floresta (Oeste) e o complexo Dungard/Cinar/Almar (Leste).
